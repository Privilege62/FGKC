About FGKC

Fellowship with the God Kind Church is a vibrant ministry founded and led by Pastor Hilton Moyo, dedicated to raising believers who understand their identity in Christ and live the God-kind life through faith in God’s Word. Our church is a place where the Gospel of Jesus Christ is preached with clarity, power, and practical relevance for everyday life. Through passionate worship, prayer, and the teaching of Scripture, we seek to equip believers to walk in victory, experience the reality of the Holy Spirit, and grow in spiritual maturity. At FGKC we believe the church is more than a meeting place—it is a spiritual family where lives are transformed, destinies are shaped, and people are empowered to live with purpose, faith, and the undeniable glory of God.

Meet Our Pastor

Pastor Hilton Moyo

Pastor Hilton Moyo is a visionary leader dedicated to teaching the undiluted Word of God. With a heart for the people and a passion for the Holy Spirit, he leads FGKC with integrity, helping believers discover their identity in Christ and walk in God’s undeniable glory.
